Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

Net Test is designed with privacy as a priority. The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification data to external servers.

2. Location Data

To find the nearest test server for accurate results, the app may request access to your location. This data is processed locally and in real-time; it is not stored or shared with the developer.

3. Local History

Your speed test history (Download, Upload, Ping, and Jitter) is stored locally on your device only. You have the option to clear this history at any time within the app settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy while using Alomory, please contact us at Alomorynet@gmail.com.
